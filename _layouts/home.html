Preamble

This is a site dedicated to all things technology, and a little bit about me. If you've manage to stumble onto this treasure trove of randomness by accident or misfortune, welcome! Please sit back and peruse at your leisure.

I've had the fortune of working in the tech industry for the better part of the last decade. My journey started off earnestly, working as a co-op student at a small NGO in Toronto. It was there I dove head first into the web programming, which relatively new to me, considering my University courses focused on software development.

I didn't know what I wanted to do in terms of a career, or where I'd end up geographically, or even if I wanted to finish school. But ultimately, over the course of interships, co-op placements, part time, contract and full time work (and graduating), I've come to love building, creating and coding software, regardless of platform.

Present Day

These days, I'm working at UBC , a world-renowned educational institution. It's here that I program solutions used by tens of thousands of students and faculty, with a unique tech stack and an awesome team. Outside of of that, I also take part in a variety of my own projects and contracts. From working with companies across various industries (  Insurance , SaaS , real estate ) to building my own software.

Personally, I find the most rewarding jobs are challenging, diverse and look to solve something meaningful. If your organization is looking for a developer proficient with all things JS, Python, and more as team lead, contact me at via email.
