Preface

This is a site dedicated to all things technology, and a little bit about me. If you've manage to stumble onto this treasure trove of randomness by accident or misfortune, welcome! Please sit back and peruse at your leisure.

How I started

I've had the fortune of working in the tech industry for the better part of the last decade. My journey started off earnestly, working as a co-op student at a small NGO in Toronto. It was there I honed my PHP, and general web development. Its there I learned that web development doesn't actually mean web programming and the world of all things web was much more complicated than I had known.

I didn't know what I wanted to do in terms of a career, or where I'd end up geographically, or even if I wanted to finish school. But ultimately, over the course of interships, co-op placements, part time, contract and full time work, I've come to love building, creating and coding software.

The present

These days, I'm working at UBC , a world-renowned educational institution. It's here that I program solutions used by tens of thousands of students and faculty, with a unique tech stack and an awesome team. Outside of of that, I also take part in a variety of my own projects and contracts. From working with companies across various industries ( Insurance , SaaS , Real estate ) to building my own software.
